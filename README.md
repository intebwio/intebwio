The browser that writes itself. 
Search for anything, and if it doesn't 
exist, we'll build the page for you
instantly.

Intebwio is an attempt at creating 
the search engine of the next generation.
Unlike classic search engines, you 
don't need to visit ten different 
links—all information is synthesized 
into one complete page. Unlike private
AI chatbots, these pages are public;
you can copy and send the link to 
anyone without them needing to log in.
And crucially, our engine smartly 
finds and serves previously generated 
topics from our database to prevent 
redundant API usage, creating a 
wildly efficient, collaborative 
knowledge base.

The internet was designed as a library,
but it has become a billboard. Today, 
searching for information means wading 
through SEO-optimized filler, 
intrusive advertisements, and fragmented
articles spread across dozens of tabs.

Intebwio was born from a radical
question: What if the browser didn't 
search for existing pages, but wrote
the perfect page for you on demand?

By leveraging state-of-the-art
Large Language Models, Intebwio acts as a
personal researcher, writer, and designer.
It understands your intent, gathers the facts, 
and synthesizes them into a beautiful, ad-free, 
highly structured article in seconds.
